GAME MAP & LOGIC

Game Structure

  • World System: The game progresses through Worlds (1, 2, 3...). Each World has 4 Levels.
  • Levels:
    • Level 1: Basic speed. Green Koopas spawn.
    • Level 2: Speed increases. Red Flying Koopas appear.
    • Level 3: Lakitu appears! Spawns Spinys.
    • Level 4 (BOSS): Boss Battle. Survive and deal damage by clearing lines!
  • Bonus Rounds:
    • Slot Machine: Triggered by stomping 3+ enemies at once or beating a level. Grind extra lives!
    • Coin Heaven: Accessible vertically (upcoming feature).

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Stomping: Drop a block onto an enemy to smash them. Combos grant spins.
  • Tetris Streaks:
    • Clear 4 lines at once for a TETRIS.
    • Chain them for DOUBLE TETRIS (x2), MASTER (x3), and GOAT (x4) titles.
  • Controls: Arrow Keys to move/rotate. Space for Hard Drop.
